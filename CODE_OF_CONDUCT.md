# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in StackDock a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept responsibility for impact.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.
- Respect maintainers' time and decisions.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, doxxing, spam, or other conduct that makes the community unsafe or unwelcoming is not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.
